DUCKBILL PLATYPUS

The sting of platypus is not dangerous to humans, but is extremely painful and causes rapid swelling in the stung area.

Scientific Name: Ornithorhynchus anatinus
Average Length: 50 cms
Average Lifespan: 17 years
Habitat: Eastern Australia and Tasmania

The unusual appearance of this egg-laying, duck-billed, beaver-tailed, otter-footed mammal baffled European naturalists when they first encountered it. It is one of the few species of venomous mammals: the male platypus has a spur on the hind foot that ddelivers a venom capable of causing severe pain to humans.
